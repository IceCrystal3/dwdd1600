Benefits of Running

There are many benefits to running, and we are going to learn about just a few of them today.

Physical Benefits

  1. Weight control
  2. Builds strong bones, muscles, and joints
  3. Helps maintain blood pressure
  4. Reduces risk of chronic illness

Mental Health Benefits

  1. Reduces stress
  2. Reduces anxiety
  3. Reduces symptoms of depression
  4. Boosts confidence

Scientific Benefits

  1. Boosts bone health
  2. Improves brain health
  3. Increase your lactate threshold
